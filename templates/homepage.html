Welcome to PSLEMCQs.com!

Hi, I am a P6 student in Taonan. I built this with help from my Dad to help fellow P6 students prepare for their PSLE. Currently, there are only English questions.

The inspiration of this site was when I realised that mistakes is how we learn, but tracking wrong questions were very tedious. So this website makes it easy to do questions and track the wrong ones for easy reflection and learning.

Credits for questions goes to all the wonderful primary schools in Singapore and their great teachers, and sites like sgtestpaper for gathering the papers.

Or you can directly register or login to be able to easily access your attempts and mistakes!
